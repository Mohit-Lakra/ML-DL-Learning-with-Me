# Inner product spaces
Inner product is a generalized version of the dot product.

An Inner Product $\langle \mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v} \rangle$ is any function that satisfies four rules:
1. Positivity: $\langle \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{v} \rangle \ge 0$.
2. Symmetry: $\langle \mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v} \rangle = \langle \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{u} \rangle$.
3. Additivity: $\langle \mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w} \rangle = \langle \mathbf{u}, \mathbf{w} \rangle + \langle \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w} \rangle$.
4. Homogeneity: $\langle c\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v} \rangle = c\langle \mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v} \rangle$.
